Waxing

Waxing is a method of semi-permanent hair removal which removes the hair from the root. The effects last for between 2-6 weeks – depending on the individual’s rate of hair growth, the time of year and how long you have been having the area waxed.

Warm wax is applied to the skin in a thin layer. The hair becomes embedded in the wax and a strip is applied and then pulled off quickly, in the opposite direction of the hair growth, taking the hair with it.

For smaller, delicate areas such as eyebrow shape, lip or chin an alternative method of tweezing could be used.

We offer a wide range of waxing services including:

  • Bikini Waxing
  • Body Waxing
  • Facial Waxing
