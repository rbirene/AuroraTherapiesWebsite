There's something indescribably magical about the Co. Down coast. Perched on the shore amongst rolling grounds, shaded by trees with views over Belfast Lough, Aurora Complementary Therapies effortlessly sets the scene for an experience less ordinary.

Our sumptuous interiors are carefully designed to remove all cares and to encourage guests to immerse themselves in the atmosphere of relaxation. Unobtrusive and calm, the staff are elegantly trained to make your stay as memorable as possible. Just as importantly though, there's endless opportunity for comfortable solitude for anyone seeking time out.
