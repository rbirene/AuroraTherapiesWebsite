The Bowen Technique

The Bowen Technique is a hands on therapy that involves gentle movement of the bodys muscles, tendons and ligaments. There is no manipulation or adjustment of hard tissue, and the treatment is gentle and relaxing. The therapist uses the thumbs and fingers to apply firm but gentle pressure to certain areas of the body. The touch is firm but not painful and the treatment can be done through light clothing.

The Bowen Technique is ideal for sports injuries, repetitive strain injuries, frozen shoulders, tension headache, migranes, asthma, hay fever, bedwetting and M.E.
